ReNew Canada Career Fair

 

The ReNew Canada Career Fair

It is an event that will connect engineering firms who work on Canada’s Top 100 Infrastructure Projects with third and fourth-year engineering students looking to work with some of the most engaging and innovative firms operating in Canada. The event is scheduled to take place on January 30, 2024 at The Toronto Reference Library.

This event is not your typical career fair. Students will have the opportunity to attend a series of short presentations on innovative projects selected from ReNew Canada’s annual Top100 Projects report. Engineering firms will provide an overview of the projects, highlight innovations, demonstrate the role that engineers played in the project, and discuss specific opportunities – internships, co-op placements, and full-time careers available within their organizations. Students will have the opportunity to speak one-on-one with presenters and other engineering firms at their booths throughout the day

The event is being organized by ReNew Canada in partnership with Ontario Society Of Professional Engineers

    About the Top100 Projects Report:
    
    ReNew Canada publishes an annual report on the 100 largest public sector infrastructure projects currently in development in Canada. The report contains details on each of the projects, including location, cost, project overview, estimated date of completion, and a list of the key companies involved with the project. Attendees can expect to hear about projects like Canada’s Top 10:
  • Site C Clean Energy Project $16M
  • Bruce Power Refurbishment $13M
  • Darlington Nuclear Refurbishment $12.8M
  • Eglinton Crosstown LRT $12.571M
  • Ontario Line $10.9M
  • GO Expansion Projects – Early Work $10.513M
  • Iona Island Wastewater Treatment Plant $9.9M
  • TTC Vehicles Program $7.175M
  • Réseau express métropolitain $6.9M
